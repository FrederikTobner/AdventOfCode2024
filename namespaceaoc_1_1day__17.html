Operation codes for the virtual machine instructions.

Enumerator
ADV 

Advance register A by division.

BXL 

XOR operation on register B with literal.

BST 

Store value in register B.

JNZ 

Jump if not zero.

BXC 

XOR operation between registers B and C.

OUT 

Output operation.

BDV 

Divide A and store in B.

CDV 

Divide A and store in C.
